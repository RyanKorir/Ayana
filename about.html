It's all in the details

A one-stop shop for beauty, self-care & everyday essentials

Ayana Hill was built around a simple idea: everyone deserves easy access to quality beauty and self-care products, without having to run all over town to find them. From fragrances and hair care to skincare, body care and sandals, we curate every item on our shelves with the same question in mind — would we be proud to use this ourselves?

We're a proudly local Kericho business, and every visit — in-store or via countrywide delivery — is a chance for us to help you look good, feel great, and enjoy the details that make the difference.
